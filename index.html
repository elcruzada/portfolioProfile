A little about me. . .

The allure of software engineering lies in the power to create. It serves as an outlet for my creative bent and enables me to engage in logical problem-solving. Whether it is crafting frontend features or developing intricate backend routes, there is a distinct joy in building something tangible from lines of code.

I am addicted to the process of creation and want to be a part of the ever-evolving world of technology. When I worked as a writer, this led me to wanting to write for cryptocurrency media companies. However, as close as this got me to technology, I further want to contribute to technology's future. Recognizing this and the more it gnawed on me, I embarked on a mission to learn programming and commit to it fully. As I began my software engineering journey, I applied and was accepted to App Academy. The decision to join such an intensive program was driven by a desire to actively contribute to the technological advances I had previously been writing about. I wanted to go beyond observing and reporting; I wanted to be a hands-on participant in the technologies of our era.

Beyond coding, reading and writing poetry is my main mode of artistic expression. I also love to travel and spent nearly four years abroad working as an ESL teacher and writer. My other interests include chess, board games, and video games. One day, I want to get a scuba diving license.
